QUIZZLE:

Content Based Quiz Generator

Quizzle is designed to make engaging quizzes easier than ever before. Our application utilizes state-of-the-art technology to analyze the content of uploaded PDFs and texts, generating quiz questions based on the material. But that's not all - we also incorporate maths problems to test users' numerical abilities.

Features & Benefits

Features

  • Eenables users to convert documents into quizzes without the need to manually copy and paste the text.

  • The system has the capability to generate questions on technical subjects such as mathematics and physics.

  • The QUIZZLE platform is gamified to incorporate a reward system, incentivizing students to perform well on quizzes and increase their motivation.

Benefits

  • Users have the ability to create questions of various types such as MCQ, Fill in the blanks, True/False, and short descriptive, based on their specific requirements.
  • Upon completion of the quiz, users can assess their performance, identify areas of weakness, and take measures to improve upon them.
  • QUIZZLE offers a convenient and accessible way for users to test their knowledge and track their progress over time.
